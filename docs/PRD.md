## 1. Product Overview

### Vision
BeQuizzy is an AI-native career growth and hiring platform that turns soft-skill-heavy commercial talent into structured, trusted hiring evidence.

The product starts with **Sales / Business Development** roles, where employers struggle to judge communication, persuasion, judgment, and coachability from CVs alone, and where candidates need repeated practice rather than one-off tests.

### Product Thesis
Most hiring for non-technical roles is still driven by:
- weak CV signals
- inconsistent interviews
- subjective gut feel
- poor visibility into junior candidate potential

At the same time, most candidates do not have a strong place to practice real hiring situations weekly. Existing assessment tools are often transactional, employer-owned, and not designed to help candidates improve.

**BeQuizzy wins by combining both sides:**
1. a candidate-side habit loop for continuous practice and improvement
2. an employer-side evidence layer for faster, less biased hiring

### Core Promise
For candidates:
> Practice realistic hiring scenarios, get deep AI feedback, improve every week, and build a verified profile that increases interview chances.

For employers:
> Screen faster, reduce weak interviews, and identify junior commercial talent with real potential instead of relying only on CVs.

## 2. Beachhead Market

### Initial Wedge
**Role family:** Sales / Business Development  
**Candidate segment:** early-career, junior, graduate, and career-switching commercial talent  
**Buyer segment:** SMEs hiring sales/BD internally

### Why this wedge first
- Communication, objection handling, and judgment are hard to assess from CVs.
- SMEs feel hiring pain quickly and decide faster than enterprises.
- Candidate demand is naturally high because sales roles are numerous and accessible.
- The role is broad enough to scale, but narrow enough to score consistently.

## 3. North Star and Core Metrics

### North Star Metric
**Success Matches per Month**  
Number of candidates who secure a meaningful hiring outcome through the platform after completing at least one verified BeQuizzy assessment module.

### First hiring outcomes to optimize
1. internship placement
2. full-time placement

### Supporting Metrics
#### Candidate side
- Weekly active candidates
- Assessment completion rate by module
- Return rate within 7 days
- % of candidates improving at least one core score over 30 days
- % of candidates completing 3+ modules

#### Employer side
- Time-to-shortlist
- Interview-to-offer efficiency improvement
- % of shortlisted candidates sourced through verified evidence profile
- Employer repeat usage rate

#### Marketplace health
- Candidate-to-job ratio by role and geography
- Interview request rate per verified profile
- Match conversion from shortlist to interview

## 4. Primary Users

### Candidate Persona A — Junior Sales Candidate
- Wants a first SDR/BD role
- Has motivation but weak interview preparation
- Needs clear feedback, practice, and proof
- Values visible progress and recruiter exposure

### Candidate Persona B — Career Switcher
- Coming from retail, service, support, or another adjacent function
- Has transferable communication ability but weak formal sales interview readiness
- Needs confidence, structure, and credible evidence

### Employer Persona A — SME Founder / Hiring Manager
- Hiring 1–5 salespeople
- Cannot spend hours screening weak applicants
- Wants a practical way to detect coachable junior talent
- Cares about speed, signal quality, and hiring confidence

### Employer Persona B — Sales Team Lead
- Needs more consistent hiring criteria across applicants
- Wants structured comparison between finalists
- Needs evidence beyond “this person talks well in interviews”

## 5. Candidate Experience

### 5.1 Continuous Practice Loop
The candidate journey is built around a repeatable loop:

**mock assessment → scorecard → weakness diagnosis → next drill → visible progress**

This must feel closer to a career gym than a one-time exam.

### 5.2 Assessment Format
BeQuizzy v1 uses a **modular multimodal assessment system**.

Each candidate completes short modules over time instead of one long exam.

#### Modalities
- voice
- video
- text
- mixed-response tasks

#### Why modular
- lowers friction
- improves completion rate
- supports repeated practice
- builds a richer longitudinal profile
- makes improvement visible, not just current level

### 5.3 Signature Assessment Areas
#### A. AI roleplay call
Simulated screening or sales call where the candidate must:
- introduce themselves clearly
- discover needs
- respond under pressure
- handle objections
- move the conversation forward

#### B. Situational judgment test
Role-specific commercial situations such as:
- unhappy prospect
- budget objection
- unclear requirement
- low-response pipeline
- awkward follow-up scenario

#### C. Presentation / pitch test
Short presentation or pitch where the candidate must:
- frame a problem
- explain a solution or value proposition
- present clearly and persuasively
- adapt tone to the target audience

#### D. Communication layer
Cross-cutting scoring on:
- clarity
- confidence
- structure
- listening / response relevance
- English communication where required

### 5.4 Candidate Outputs
After each module, the candidate receives:
- a structured scorecard
- strengths and weaknesses
- highlighted mistakes or missed opportunities
- an improvement recommendation
- the next suggested drill

### 5.5 Candidate Profile
Each candidate accumulates a **Verified Evidence Profile** containing:
- latest scores by competency
- progress trend over time
- completed modules
- highlighted strengths
- risk flags
- selected evidence snippets or clips
- readiness level for target role

This profile is the product’s main trust artifact.

## 6. Employer Experience

### 6.1 Core Employer Value
BeQuizzy should help employers:
1. filter weak candidates faster
2. reduce bad hires caused by weak signal quality
3. spot junior candidates with strong upside

### 6.2 Employer Workflow
#### Step 1 — Define hiring target
Employer selects role context such as:
- SDR
- telesales
- B2B business development
- account executive (junior)

#### Step 2 — Receive candidate shortlist
Candidates are ranked by:
- current readiness
- communication quality
- objection handling
- situational judgment
- presentation strength
- coachability / growth velocity

#### Step 3 — Inspect evidence
Instead of raw scores only, employers see:
- competency breakdown
- why the score is high or low
- evidence excerpts
- improvement trend
- recommended interview focus areas

#### Step 4 — Use BeQuizzy across the funnel
BeQuizzy supports the full funnel:
- pre-screening
- shortlist comparison
- finalist validation

### 6.3 Employer Dashboard Requirements
- role-fit ranking
- candidate comparison view
- score explanation layer
- interview risk flags
- shortlist export / share
- candidate pipeline notes

## 7. Core Competency Model

### Commercial Talent Competencies for v1
1. communication clarity
2. persuasion and value framing
3. objection handling
4. situational judgment
5. problem solving
6. presentation ability
7. confidence and composure
8. coachability / growth velocity

### Important principle
BeQuizzy should not pretend to measure personality in a vague or pseudoscientific way. It should focus on:
- observable behavior
- repeatable scenarios
- explainable scoring
- evidence the employer can inspect

## 8. MVP Scope

### Candidate-side MVP
- onboarding with target role selection
- modular multimodal assessments
- AI scorecards per module
- personalized drill recommendations
- verified evidence profile
- weekly practice loop

### Employer-side MVP
- SME account
- role-based candidate shortlist
- candidate evidence profile viewer
- comparison view for finalists
- notes and lightweight shortlist management

### Commercial MVP
- free candidate core
- paid employer access
- optional candidate premium later

### Explicitly out of scope for v1
- all job families at once
- enterprise ATS replacement
- full psychometric testing suite
- complex university administration workflows
- broad marketplace across every non-technical role

## 11. Success Criteria for v1

### Candidate success
- Candidate completes first module in under 15 minutes
- Candidate understands their top 2 weaknesses immediately
- Candidate sees meaningful progress after 2–3 sessions
- Candidate returns weekly for practice

### Employer success
- Employer can review stronger signal than CV within minutes
- Employer can compare finalists on structured competencies
- Employer feels more confident hiring junior candidates

### Product success
- Repeat candidate usage proves habit loop
- Employers repeatedly use scorecards in real hiring decisions
- First paid customers come from SMEs hiring sales talent

## 12. Future Expansion

Once Sales/BD wedge is validated, BeQuizzy can expand into adjacent non-technical job families:
- customer support / customer success
- recruiter / talent acquisition
- marketing / content / growth
- account management
- operations roles with communication-heavy workflows

Expansion should happen by **job family playbooks**, not by launching generic assessments for everyone.
